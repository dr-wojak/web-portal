Précis

The Meinhardt Initiative documents a nineteen-year failure of the Irish State to evaluate, determine, or formally respond to Operations Research materials first submitted in 2006.

Across six Taoisigh and multiple statutory authorities, the administrative output has remained invariant: **no determination**.

The record describes a closed administrative system termed the Nullification Loop .
